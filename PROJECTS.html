Project name SEISMIC ANALYSIS AND DESIGN OF RESIDENTIAL BUILDINGS WITH DAMPERS USING ETABS.
Duration August 2019 to August 2020
Software used ETABS
Description • In this project (G+10, G+15, and G+20) storey RCC buildings have been chosen and modeled with friction, visco-elastic and viscous dampers.
• Analysis of top storey displacement, inter-storey drift, base shear, and stiffness of the buildings has been compared with original buildings.
• Damper has been chosen for improving the seismic strength of reinforced concrete building.
